Installation

Download file game.exe on the right in the releases section.

The file was compiled on 64 bit OS Windows 10, that is, it will not run on 32 bit OS Windows 7.

For errors/questions, you can also read here

How to play?

Before you start using it, it is recommended to change the background to black in the console.

See instructions here

At launch, authorization or registration of an account is offered, which means that once you create an account, you can use it an unlimited number of times.

Action "About the game" - provides an opportunity to read basic information about the game.

The action "Exit the game" - speaks for itself.

Once you've logged in/registered, you don't need to do anything else. As said the game is automatic or "self-playing".

Need to exit?

Just close it. All data will be saved.

ATTENTION: REMEMBER YOUR ACCOUNT AUTHORIZATION DATA.
IN THE EVENT OF ACCOUNT AUTHORIZATION DATA LOSS, IT WILL NOT BE POSSIBLE TO RECOVERY.

Also, in case of loss of account authorization data, you have the opportunity to register a new account.

Registration and ownership of multiple accounts is permitted; registration of new accounts is limited.

Plot

Another world, which is called "Krominong", needs to be saved from the forces of evil. Adventurers are being summoned from all over Krominong.
Are you ready to become one of them?
Can you climb the 100th floor of the Sky Labyrinth and eradicate the evil in Crominong?

The target audience

Office workers, high school students and students with free access to a computer, as well as the dream of becoming an adventurer, regardless of gender.
